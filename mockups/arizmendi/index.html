Our Story

Baked with care,
owned by everyone.

Arizmendi Bakery isn't owned by a CEO or investors — it's owned by every person who works here. Since 2000, we've been baking in the Inner Sunset with a simple belief: great bread comes from people who care about what they make, and who share in what they build. Every loaf, every pastry, every pizza is made with that intention.
